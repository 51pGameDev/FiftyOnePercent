FIFTYONE PERCENT Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy pertains to the mobile game “Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” developed and provided by “FIFTYONE PERCENT.”

1. Personal Information Collection

“Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” does not collect any personal information from users. The game does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

2. Collection of Non-Personal Data

Within the game, non-personal data, such as user gameplay activity and puzzle-solving records, may be collected to enhance the gaming experience and gather statistical information. This information is non-identifiable and is not used for identifying or tracking individual users.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The “Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” game does not involve the collection of personal information related to advertising or third-party services within the game. If users click on in-game ads or use third-party services, the privacy policies of those service providers apply.

4. Protection of Minors’ Personal Information

“Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” is intended for children and minors, and we make every effort to protect the privacy of minors. When minors use the game, we comply with legal protections and require parental or guardian consent.

5. Personal Information Security

We take reasonable security measures to protect user personal information and non-personal data, ensuring the safety of game data. However, please understand that complete security cannot be guaranteed in an online environment.

6. Access, Modification, and Deletion of Personal Information

As “Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” does not collect personal information, there is no need for users to request access, modification, or deletion of personal information.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be communicated through in-game announcements. By continuing to use the game after such changes, users are considered to have agreed to the updated policy.

“Dream Piece Puzzle Friends” takes user privacy seriously and strictly adheres to this privacy policy. User data is protected, ensuring a safe gaming experience. If you have any additional questions or concerns about the privacy policy, please contact us at the email address below.
